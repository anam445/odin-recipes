Carrot Ribbon Salad

This carrot ribbon salad, with a tangy dressing and bacon, is quick to shake together in a jar, and the ribbon-cut carrots make a beautiful presentation.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 4 carrots, peeled into wide ribbons using a vegetable peeler (about 4 cups)
  • 1/3 cup crumbled cooked bacon
  • 1/3 cup thinly sliced shallots
  • 3 tablespoons dried sweetened cranberries
  • 3 tablespoons honey-roasted pecan pieces
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley, plus more for garnish

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all ingredients.

  2. step 2

    3. Whisk together mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, and salt in a 1-quart container with a lid.

  3. Step 3

    4. Add carrots, bacon, shallots, cranberries, pecan pieces, and parsley; cover and shake until carrots are evenly coated in mayonnaise mixture.

  4. Step 4

    5. Transfer to a serving bowl and garnish with parsley.
